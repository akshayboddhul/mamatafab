Who We Are

A story About Us

Boddul Family has its origin from a small village from Kondur(Warangal) Andhra Pradesh.

In 1970, Satayanarayan Boddul opned a shop in Sakar Peth, Solapur to sell our chadders with brand name Swati, Ashwini, Surbhi.

Mamata Fabrics earned a goodwill in this business because of the quality we provided.

In the Year of 2000, We opened showroom as S. Boddul Textiles Showroom.
